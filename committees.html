Committees

Featured Committees

DISEC

The Disarmament and International Security Committee focuses on global security, arms control, and threats to peace.

WHO

The World Health Organisation addresses international public health challenges and coordinates efforts to combat global health crises.

UNHRC

The United Nations Human Rights Council promotes and protects human rights around the world.

Lok Sabha

Simulates the Indian Parliament, offering delegates a chance to debate domestic and international policies.

International Press (IP)

Acts as journalists and media houses, reporting, analyzing, and questioning delegates during sessions.

UNOOSA

Focuses on peaceful exploration and use of outer space, and developing international cooperation in space law.

HCC

Fast-paced crisis simulation inspired by supernatural events in Hawkins, from Stranger Things.

UNODC

Addresses global issues related to organized crime, illicit drugs, corruption, and terrorism.
