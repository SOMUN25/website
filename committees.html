Featured Committees

UNSC

The United Nations Security Council is charged with maintaining international peace and security. Delegates address crises and conflicts that challenge global stability.

Sample Agenda: Addressing escalating tensions in the South China Sea

UNHRC

The United Nations Human Rights Council works to promote and protect human rights around the world, ensuring accountability and justice for all.

Sample Agenda: Protecting the rights of refugees in conflict zones

DISEC

The Disarmament and International Security Committee focuses on issues related to global security, arms control, and threats to peace.

Sample Agenda: Regulating the use of autonomous weapons in warfare

ECOSOC

The Economic and Social Council coordinates international efforts to promote sustainable development and economic cooperation.

Sample Agenda: Advancing global strategies for climate resilience

Lok Sabha

The Lok Sabha committee simulates the Indian Parliament, offering delegates a chance to debate domestic and international policies from a national perspective.

Sample Agenda: Addressing cybersecurity threats to national security
