Committees

Featured Committees

DISEC

The Disarmament and International Security Committee focuses on issues related to global security, arms control, and threats to peace.

WHO

The World Health Organisation addresses international public health challenges and coordinates efforts to combat global health crises.

UNHRC

The United Nations Human Rights Council works to promote and protect human rights around the world, ensuring accountability and justice for all.

Lok Sabha

The Lok Sabha committee simulates the Indian Parliament, offering delegates a chance to debate domestic and international policies from a national perspective.

International Press (IP)

The International Press plays the role of journalists and media houses, reporting, analyzing, and questioning delegates during committee sessions.

UNOOSA

The United Nations Office for Outer Space Affairs focuses on the peaceful exploration and use of outer space, and developing international cooperation in space law and exploration.

HCC

The Hawkins Crisis Committee is a fast-paced crisis simulation inspired by supernatural events in Hawkins, drawing from the Stranger Things universe.

UNODC

The United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime addresses global issues related to organized crime, illicit drugs, corruption, and terrorism.
