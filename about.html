About SOMUN

Our Mission

At SOMUN, our mission is to provide a platform where young leaders can engage in meaningful debate, foster diplomatic dialogue, and cultivate the skills necessary to address global challenges with confidence and integrity.

Our Vision

We envision SOMUN as a forum that shapes tomorrow’s leaders today—where students not only learn about international relations and policy-making but also develop the empathy, collaboration, and leadership needed to create a better world.

Conference Legacy

Since its inception, SOMUN has grown into one of India’s most respected Model United Nations conferences. With diverse committees, distinguished chairs, and participants from across the nation, SOMUN has established itself as a hub for excellence in debate and diplomacy.

Why SOMUN?

  • Engage in dynamic debates modeled after real-world UN committees.
  • Gain exposure to global issues and practical problem-solving.
  • Network with like-minded peers and experienced mentors.
  • Enhance leadership, public speaking, and negotiation skills.
