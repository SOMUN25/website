About SOMUN 2025

SOMUN (Student Organization Model United Nations) 2025 is a prestigious MUN conference where delegates from across the world gather to discuss global issues and engage in diplomatic negotiations. Hosted annually, the event brings together young leaders who are passionate about creating solutions for today's most pressing challenges. As an international conference, SOMUN provides a platform for participants to enhance their public speaking, critical thinking, and leadership skills, while fostering cross-cultural understanding and cooperation.
