About SOMUN

Our Vision

SOMUN stands as a beacon of diplomatic excellence, fostering tomorrow's leaders through engaging dialogue and meaningful debate. Our conference provides a platform for young minds to tackle global challenges, develop critical thinking skills, and forge lasting connections.

Our History

Since its inception, SOMUN has grown to become one of India's most prestigious Model United Nations conferences. Hosted by Silver Oaks International School, we bring together diverse perspectives and talented individuals from across the nation.

What Sets Us Apart

At SOMUN, we believe in creating an environment that goes beyond traditional debate. Our conference emphasizes practical diplomacy, real-world problem-solving, and the development of leadership skills that last a lifetime.
